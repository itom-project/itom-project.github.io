“itom1DQWTFigure” is the basic line plot for plotting DataObjects with 1xN or Mx1 data-Slices based on QWT. All DataTypes are accepted. To plot complex objects, it is possible to select between the following modes: “absolut”, “phase”, “real” and “imaginary”.
