57 /*
58 Adds support for word click events.
59 
60 It will highlight the click-able word when the user press control and move
61 the mouse over a word.
62 
63 Detecting whether a word is click-able is the responsability of the
64 subclasses. You must override ``_check_word_cursor`` and call
65 ``_select_word_cursor`` if this is a click-able word (this
66 process might be asynchrone) otherwise _clear_selection.
67 
68 :attr:`pyqode.core.modes.WordClickMode.word_clicked` is emitted
69 when the word is clicked by the user (while keeping control pressed).
70 */
