1.19. QCam¶

Summary:

Firewire QCam cameras from QImaging

Type:

DataIO

License:

itom-plugin under LGPL / QCam driver under ???

Platforms:

Windows, (Linux possible but yet not implemented)

Devices:

Cameras from company QImaging

Author:

M. Holtom, College of Eng., Swansea University; M. Gronle, ITO, University Stuttgart

1.19.1. Overview¶

This plugin is currently under development.

1.19.3. Parameters¶

An instance of this plugin has the following internal parameters:

name: {str}, read-only

QCam

integration_time: {float}

Integrationtime of CCD programmed in s

gain: {float}

Gain

offset: {float}

Offset

x0: {int}

first pixel of ROI in x-direction

y0: {int}

first pixel of ROI in y-direction

x1: {int}

last pixel of ROI in x-direction

y1: {int}

last pixel of ROI in y-direction

sizex: {int}, read-only

width of ROI

sizey: {int}, read-only

height of ROI

bpp: {int}

bit depth per pixel

cooled: {int}

CCD cooler
