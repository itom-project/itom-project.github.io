Speckle¶

Simple waveoptical simulations can be easily performed using the matrix computation capabilities of itom in combination with perfect visualization options.

Here you see the simulation of a speckle pattern that one obtains on a camera when imaging a rough surface illuminated by coherent light. The average “size” of the speckles depends on the F-number of the imaging system (and the wavelength).
